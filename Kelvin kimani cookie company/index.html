Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Butter or Margarine softened
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 2 eggs, large
  • 2 tea spoons vanilla
  • 1/4 cups of flour unsifted
  • 1 tea spoon baking soda
  • 1/2 tea spoon salt
  • 1 cup walnuts or pecan
  • 2 cups Kcc bitter sweet

Baking instractions

  1. Heat oven to 375℉
  2. Stir flour with bakingsoda and salt set aside
  3. In a large mixing bowl, beat butter with sugar,
    and brown suger at medium speed until creamy and lightened in color
  4. add eggs and vanilla, one at a time.
    mix on low speed until incorporated.
  5. Gradually blend dry mixture into creamed mixture.
    Stir in the nuts and chocolate chips
  6. Drop by tabelspoon onto ungreased cookie sheets.
  7. Bake for 9 to 11 minutes or until the chocolate chipcookies are golden brown.
  8. Enjoy!!
